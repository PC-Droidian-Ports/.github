## Hi there 👋

Given the current situation with Planet, we have taken the option to merge all
community-developed (except `Cosmo-CoDiOS`, and `Gemian`) [unofficial] Planet
organisations into one organisation.

All of the repositories in this organisation, on the 4th of January, will be
consolidated into one new organisation.

Effectively, due to lack of communication and support for this vibrant
community, the decision has been taken to a) consolidate organisations, b)
enable donations on one single organisation-wide setup, c) begin to sunset
efforts until such time that our efforts are recognised.

However, this doesn't mean we won't accept PRs or other enthusiants joining the
community.

This new organisation can be found
[here](https://github.com/planet-community-org) - and our new website will be
up soon. Save your [bookmarks](https://planet-community.org).

It has been a difficult decision to make, but the spirit still remains alive.

See you there, and thanks for all the fish!
